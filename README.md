<h3>About</h3>

<p>
Industrial PhD Researcher (2025–2028) at Deep Insight designing scalable data architectures and production-grade machine learning systems for maritime and IoT environments.
</p>

<p>
Research Team Member of the NEUROCOMP-IA project at Universidad Autónoma de Madrid, focused on bio-inspired neural networks, sparse coding, and adaptive learning dynamics.
</p>

<p>
