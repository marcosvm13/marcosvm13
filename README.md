## Research Focus

I work at the intersection of bio-inspired neural computation, sparse coding, adaptive threshold dynamics and industrial AI deployment.

My research connects computational neuroscience principles with scalable machine learning architectures designed for real-world environments.

**Neural Threshold Adaptation and Sparse Coding Strategies**

Published in IEEE Xplore

Industrial PhD researcher (2025–2028) working on:

- Vessel dynamics analytics (Surge · Sway · Yaw)  
- Operational risk modeling  
- High-frequency telemetry data engineering  
- Production-grade ML serving systems  

Most projects are proprietary and deployed in industrial environments.
